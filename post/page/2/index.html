I'm a progressive because

I believe that left to their our devices humans could veer towards inhumanity by unleashing their inner demons. History has taught us than humans can unleash horror. The nazis weren’t aliens from another planet. They were human. And these humans devised and carried out a massive plan of torture and extermination. This historic incident repeated itself in various forms and different levels of intensity with the Stalin purges, Pol Pot in Cambodia or even in Rwanda.
