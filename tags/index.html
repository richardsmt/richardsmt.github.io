Why so many coins ?

At the time of writing, there are 1059 cryptocurrencies listed of the coinmarketcap.com, ( the go-to website for price, market information ). The list starts with Bitcoin, Ethereum and Ripple at the top and ends with ELcoin, microCoin and Turbocoin. The question : why do many coins ? Do we really need so many different digital currencies ? Is it even conceivable that even 50 of them have a stable long term future.
