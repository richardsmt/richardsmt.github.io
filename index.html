Gaming progress and procrastination

Many games have achievement trackers that allow the player to aim for an arbitrary objective. The most sophisticated versions bundle objectives into groups that give a sense of a clear progression that hides the tedious steps required.

World of Warcraft is such and I’ve generally looked over these artificial achievements focusing instead of the more obvious level increase and zone discovery. Recently however things have changed and I’ve manage to go through several layers of repetitive game play with only the achievement system as a guide.
